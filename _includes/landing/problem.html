AWS Secrets and GCP Secret Manager are also pricey and rely on frequent API calls to their services, driving up costs.

Solutions like Doppler and Infisical are also pretty pricey and sync your secrets over risky third-party integrations that increase your attack surface area, making it more likely your secrets leak someday.
