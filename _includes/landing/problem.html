Manual management scatters secrets

Your developers manage secrets independently, exchanging .env files over insecure channels like email and Slack.

Meanwhile, your DevOps team sets secrets through various different platforms and tools, resulting in secrets being spread across multiple locations.

And your CSO has no transparency into any of this. There are no audit logs, no access controls, and no preventive measures against leaks.

Enterprise solutions are expensive or risky

HashiCorp Vault locks you into an expensive solution by requiring your team to rewrite code.

AWS Secrets and GCP Secret Manager are also pricey and rely on frequent API calls to their services, driving up costs.

Solutions like Doppler and Infisical are also pretty pricey and sync your secrets over risky third-party integrations that increase your attack surface area, making it more likely your secrets leak someday.
