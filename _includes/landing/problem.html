Your developers manage secrets independently, exchanging .env files over insecure channels like email and Slack. Meanwhile, your DevOps team sets secrets through various different platforms and tools, resulting in secrets being spread across multiple locations.

Alternative solutions have limitations and risks

HashiCorp Vault can lock you into an expensive solution by requiring your team to rewrite code. Solutions like AWS Secrets and GCP Secret Manager are also pricey and rely on frequent API calls to their services, driving up costs. Doppler and Infisical mostly avoid the need for code rewrites, but they rely on risky third-party integrations that may increase your attack surface area, making it more likely for your secrets to leak.

  • × Expensive
  • × Risky third-party integrations
  • × Proprietary / Lock-in
